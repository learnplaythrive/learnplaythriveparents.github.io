Inclusion is a survival need. Because humans need each other to survive, our nervous systems can register social exclusion as a life or death threat. And as parents, we want our neurodivergent kids to feel safely encircled in authentic, joyful community. But when we’ve never seen these social spaces before, it can be so hard to even know what to advocate for and how to do it.

If you are looking for more skills to create inclusive, supportive spaces for your neurodivergent child, this free webinar is for you. Join Lindsay Teitelbaum, speech therapist and owner of Spirited Play Labs, to explore how you can use relational skills to create joy, connection, and authentic community for your neurodivergent child.
