Trainings for parents & caregivers

We know by now that trying to change a child’s behavior to make those around them more comfortable often comes at the expense of their well-being.

Our courses help you support your child to learn, play, and thrive in their real daily routines, without compromising their authentic well-being. At Learn Play Thrive, we believe that your Autistic child has the right to JOY, PLAY, ACCEPTANCE, & AUTHENTIC CONNECTION.

Our courses help you get there.

Community

Be a part of the most supportive community of parents and caregivers you’ve ever experienced. The Learn Play Thrive Parent community is a space where parents and caregivers come together to share resources, care for each others’ experiences, and have each other’s back. We are actively building a space that says no to ableism and yes to access needs and intersectionality. It’s brand new, and we want you there.
